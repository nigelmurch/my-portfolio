Why I Became a UX Researcher

I’m a UX researcher and service designer shaped by hardship, hope, and relentless belief in people.

From Southland paddocks to hospital wards, I’ve lived through systems that overlook people. That’s why I design for inclusion, equity, and understanding.

Gaming gave me freedom. My daughter gave me purpose. And now, I give everything I’ve got to user research.

“I will study again,” I promised her. And I did.

My Values
