ABOUT

“Your users have secrets, I find them.
(ethically, of course).”

Nigel Murch is a UX researcher and service designer focused on creating digital experiences that are authentic, ethical, sustainable, and intuitive.

Having a deep interest in human psychology and behaviour, Nigel explores how digital design can support – not manipulate – real people. He believes true representation goes beyond diversity checkboxes, using co-design to ensure that communities actively shape the services built for them.

Nigel creates digital experiences that are intuitive, inclusive, and sustainable – guided by real user needs and cultural insight, while challenging manipulative design by championing transparency and co-design, ensuring communities shape the services meant for them.

With a focus on ethical practices and environmental responsibility, his research-led approach turns complexity into clarity, making digital interactions feel effortless and meaningful.
