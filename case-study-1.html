Wild Boar to the Rescue

In southern Italy, wild boar populations have exploded due to climate change and habitat loss. Local foraging groups and chefs are collaborating to harvest wild boar sustainably, turning them into nutritious meals distributed through food banks. This not only reduces crop damage and road accidents, but also provides iron-rich protein to vulnerable communities.
