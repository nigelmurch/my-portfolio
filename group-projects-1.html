FLAVOURS BY PAPATŪĀNUKU

A co-design UX research project exploring food sovereignty, cultural expression, and Papatuanuku’s wisdom in the digital space.

The Problem!

The problem I wanted to solve is why people are hesitant to try alternative food sources like invasive flora and fauna, overcoming this leads to exploring the intersection of indigenous identity, seasonal kai, and storytelling.

Secondary Research

My aim is to design an interactive platform anchored in Māori values and food sovereignty movements while addressing alternative food options for eco-conscious and financially challenged users.

Initial Research

Through surveys and interviews, I captured body language and lived experiences to ground the research in real human stories.

Case Studies

I explored ethical design practices in cultural platforms and storytelling environments to inspire my own process.
