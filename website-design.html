Whetū Mārama – Rangatahi Sexual Health Bus

This project focused on reducing stigma and increasing access to sexual health services for rangatahi aged 15–25. We co-designed a mobile clinic experience including a vending machine for free testing kits and private soundproof booths for telehealth consultations.

Open Door Project – Grief and Communication

The Open Door Project explored ways to support tamariki and whānau in talking about grief and loss. Our team created a retro-inspired interactive storytelling experience with resources designed to foster safe and meaningful conversations between children and adults.
