Whetū Mārama

Whetū Mārama is a rangatahi-focused health service on wheels. Designed to reduce stigma and improve access to sexual healthcare for 15–25 year olds, this mobile clinic brings essential services to the community. The bus features a vending machine for free testing kits, interactive health resources, and private soundproof booths for telehealth sessions.
