Whetū Mārama

Whetū Mārama My Explanation of Whetū Mārama Whetū Mārama is a project we created to empower and uplift Rangatahi, especially Māori youth, by reconnecting them with their identity, whakapapa, and mātauranga through the use of digital storytelling and creative technology. It’s about putting the stars back in the eyes of our young people,helping them see themselves reflected in the stories they tell, and in the tools they use to tell them. At its heart, Whetū Mārama blends the richness of te ao Māori with the power of emerging tech — whether that’s animation, interactive media, or immersive platforms like AR and VR. It’s a space where traditional pūrākau meet the digital future. But more than that, it’s inclusive and multicultural by design. We acknowledge that our communities are diverse, and Whetū Mārama makes space for all voices, encouraging cross-cultural understanding and collective storytelling that reflects the true makeup of Aotearoa. This project isn’t just about showcasing culture, it’s about shaping futures. We want Rangatahi to see that their stories, values, and ways of knowing are not only valid but vital in a world that’s constantly evolving. By building their digital confidence, creative expression, and connection to culture, Whetū Mārama helps prepare our young people to lead, innovate, and thrive in both the digital space and in their own lives.
